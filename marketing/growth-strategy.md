## 💰 Monetization Strategy

### Revenue Stream Diversification

#### 1. Core Transaction Revenue (70% of revenue)
- **Commission**: 15% of order value (€15-20 per order)
- **Delivery Fees**: €2.90 per order (waived on €20+ orders)
- **Small Order Surcharge**: €1.50 on orders under €12

#### 2. Premium Services (15% of revenue)
- **UberFoods Premium**: €4.99/month
  - Free delivery on all orders
  - Priority customer support
  - Ad-free experience
  - Exclusive restaurant access
- **Express Delivery**: €3.99 per order
  - 20-minute delivery guarantee
  - Dedicated driver pool

#### 3. Restaurant Services (10% of revenue)
- **Restaurant Subscription**: €49/month
  - Featured placement in search
  - Advanced analytics dashboard
  - Marketing budget co-op
  - Priority driver assignment
- **Menu Optimization**: €99/month
  - AI-powered menu recommendations
  - Dynamic pricing suggestions
  - Customer preference analytics

#### 4. Advertising & Data (5% of revenue)
- **Sponsored Restaurants**: €199/month for featured slots
- **Targeted Ads**: CPM-based restaurant advertising
- **Market Insights**: Aggregated data licensing

### Pricing Strategy

#### Dynamic Pricing Model
- **Peak Hours**: +10-20% delivery fees during busy periods
- **Weather Impact**: +€1-2 during bad weather
- **Distance-Based**: Variable fees based on delivery distance
- **Demand Surge**: Algorithmic pricing during high demand

#### Psychological Pricing
- **€9.99** instead of €10 for psychological pricing
- **Free Delivery** thresholds strategically set
- **Bundle Pricing**: Meal deals, family packs
- **Loyalty Discounts**: Tiered rewards system

### Unit Economics Optimization

#### Customer Economics
- **Average Order Value (AOV)**: Target €28 (current industry €22)
- **Order Frequency**: Target 4.5 orders/month (current 3.2)
- **Customer Lifetime**: Target 18 months (current 12 months)
- **Customer Lifetime Value (LTV)**: Target €500 (current €300)

#### Restaurant Economics
- **Average Revenue per Restaurant**: Target €2,500/month
- **Restaurant Lifetime Value**: Target €50,000
- **Customer Acquisition Cost**: Target €8
- **LTV:CAC Ratio**: Target 5:1

#### Driver Economics
- **Average Earnings per Driver**: Target €800/month
- **Driver Retention Rate**: Target 85% (current 70%)
- **Driver Satisfaction Score**: Target 4.6/5
