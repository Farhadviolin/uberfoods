# 🗺️ UberFoods 2026 Roadmap: From Launch to Market Leadership

## Executive Summary

**UberFoods 2026 Roadmap** outlines the strategic path from launch to market leadership in European food delivery. Following the successful 30-day transformation from startup to enterprise-grade platform, this roadmap focuses on disciplined execution, rapid user acquisition, and sustainable scaling.

**Key Objectives:**
- **Launch Success**: 50K MAU in Vienna within 6 months
- **Revenue Growth**: €300K MRR by Q2, €1M MRR by Q4
- **Market Expansion**: Munich and Zurich launches
- **Financial Milestones**: Break-even by Q3, profitability by Q4
- **Team Scaling**: 25 → 100+ person organization
