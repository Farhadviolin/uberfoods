## 💬 Customer Support Framework

### Support Channel Strategy

#### Digital Channels (Primary - 70% of volume)
1. **In-App Support**
   - Integrated chat widget
   - Order status integration
   - Quick actions (reorder, track, contact restaurant)
   - AI-powered chatbot for basic queries

2. **Web Portal**
   - Self-service knowledge base
   - Ticket submission system
   - Order history and management
   - Account settings and preferences

3. **Social Media**
   - Facebook Messenger
   - Instagram DMs
   - Twitter/X support
   - Proactive engagement

#### Voice Channels (Secondary - 20% of volume)
1. **Phone Support**
   - Local numbers for each market
   - IVR system with intelligent routing
   - Callback scheduling for peak times
   - Multilingual support (German, English, Turkish)

2. **Video Support**
   - Complex issue resolution
   - Technical troubleshooting
   - High-value customer support

#### Offline Channels (Tertiary - 10% of volume)
1. **Email Support**
   - Formal complaint resolution
   - Legal and regulatory matters
   - Business customer support

2. **Physical Support**
   - Local offices in major cities
   - Pop-up support events
   - Partnership with restaurants

### Support Tiers & Escalation

#### Tier 1: First Line Support (80% resolution rate target)
**Response Time**: < 2 minutes (chat), < 5 minutes (phone)
**Scope**:
- Order status inquiries
- Basic account issues
- Password resets
- App troubleshooting
- General questions

**Tools**: Zendesk, Intercom, custom dashboard

#### Tier 2: Advanced Support (15% resolution rate target)
**Response Time**: < 15 minutes
**Scope**:
- Complex order issues
- Payment disputes
- Restaurant complaints
- Technical issues
- Account suspensions

**Tools**: Full CRM access, payment system access, restaurant portal

#### Tier 3: Expert Support (5% resolution rate target)
**Response Time**: < 4 hours
**Scope**:
- Legal disputes
- Fraud investigations
- Regulatory compliance
- System-level issues
- High-value customer escalations

**Tools**: Executive access, legal system integration, custom solutions

## 🚨 Crisis Management Framework

### Incident Response Process

#### Phase 1: Detection & Assessment (0-15 minutes)
1. **Incident Detection**
   - Automated monitoring alerts
   - Customer reports
   - Partner notifications
   - Team member alerts

2. **Initial Assessment**
   - Severity classification (P0-P4)
   - Impact assessment (users, revenue, reputation)
   - Root cause identification
   - Communication plan activation

#### Phase 2: Response & Mitigation (15-60 minutes)
1. **Response Team Assembly**
   - Incident commander assignment
   - Cross-functional team mobilization
   - Communication channels activation
   - Stakeholder notification

2. **Mitigation Actions**
   - System isolation and containment
   - Backup system activation
   - Manual process implementation
   - Customer communication

#### Phase 3: Resolution & Recovery (1-24 hours)
1. **Root Cause Analysis**
   - Technical investigation
   - Process review
   - Contributing factor identification
   - Solution development

2. **System Recovery**
   - Fix implementation and testing
   - Gradual system restoration
   - Performance monitoring
   - User communication

#### Phase 4: Post-Incident Review (24-72 hours)
1. **Incident Review**
   - Timeline reconstruction
   - Effectiveness assessment
   - Lesson learned documentation
   - Process improvement identification

2. **Communication & Follow-up**
   - Customer reassurance
   - Partner updates
   - Public communication if required
   - Preventive measure communication

### Crisis Communication Plan

#### Internal Communication
- **Incident Response Channel**: Dedicated Slack channel
- **Status Updates**: Every 15 minutes during active incident
- **Post-Mortem**: Within 72 hours of resolution

#### External Communication
- **Customer Communication**: Status page and app notifications
- **Partner Communication**: Dedicated partner portal updates
- **Media Communication**: Prepared statements and spokesperson
- **Public Updates**: Social media and press releases

#### Critical Business Functions
